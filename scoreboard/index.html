Here you can check your ranking based on you DagPoints® in real time!

What are DagPoints® anyway?

Glad you asked! DagPoints® are the official way to reward (and punish) people according to Dagnet's own values. Following a positive action you will receive DagPoints®, while a negative action will incur a DagPoints® loss.

Why should I care about my DagPoints® score?

You shouldn't.

My ranking is currently low, how do I change that?

You have two main options:

  • Increase your score by doing things Dagnet likes
  • Sabotage people who have better scores than you
I think we all know which is the correct choice here.
